Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was awarded the Bharat Ratna in 1997.

Mother Teresa

Mother Teresa, known in the Catholic Church as Saint Teresa of Calcutta, was an Albanian-Indian Roman Catholic nun and missionary. She was awarded the Bharat Ratna in 1980.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team. He was awarded the Bharat Ratna in 2014.
